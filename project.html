About the Prediction Model (Enhanced)

The Lifelytics model is an advanced rule-based intelligent scoring system designed to evaluate complex lifestyle algorithms and predict physiological outcomes.

  • Computes precise BMI ratios from anthropometric data
  • Applies dynamic age-based risk curve adjustments
  • Evaluates the holistic impact of exercise, rest, and neuro-stress levels
  • Considers toxicological impact (smoking and alcohol)
  • Integrates pre-existing clinical conditions (BP, diabetes, cholesterol, cardiovascular)
